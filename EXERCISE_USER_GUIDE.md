## What Data Gets Saved?

All your exercise data is saved for **at least 7 days** including:
- Exercise name
- Date and time performed
- Duration (minutes)
- Calories burned
- Intensity level (for manual logs)
- Personal notes (for manual logs)

This helps track:
- Total workouts completed
- Workout streak (consecutive days)
- Total minutes exercised
- Calories burned over time
- Progress toward your goals

## Troubleshooting

**Q: AI button not working?**
- Make sure you have internet connection
- AI needs a few seconds to analyze your data
- Wait for loading to complete

**Q: Where can I see my exercise history?**
- All exercises are saved to your profile
- Check the stats cards: Total Workouts, Streak, Total Minutes
- Data persists even if you close the app

**Q: Can I edit or delete logged exercises?**
- Currently, exercises once logged cannot be edited
- Make sure details are correct before saving

**Q: How accurate is the calorie calculation?**
- Manual logs use standard metabolic rates
- Actual calories may vary based on weight and effort
- Values are estimates for tracking purposes
