## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I do AI exercises every day?
**A**: Yes! The AI adjusts intensity to prevent overtraining. However, rest days are important - listen to your body.

### Q: What if I can't do an exercise?
**A**: No problem! Tap "SKIP" to move to the next one. The AI will learn your preferences.

### Q: Do I need equipment?
**A**: Most AI exercises use bodyweight only. If equipment is needed, it will be clearly stated (dumbbells, resistance band, etc.).

### Q: How long does a session take?
**A**: Each exercise is ~15 minutes, so a full session is about 45 minutes. You can do just one exercise if short on time!

### Q: Can I do these exercises at home?
**A**: Absolutely! All exercises are designed to be home-friendly.

### Q: What if the GIF doesn't load?
**A**: Check your internet connection. GIFs are stored locally, so they should work offline once loaded.

### Q: Are these exercises safe for beginners?
**A**: Yes! The AI considers your age and fitness level. Start slow and focus on form.

### Q: Can I redo the same exercise?
**A**: After completing a session, refresh AI for new exercises. The AI adds variety to prevent boredom.
